Our Story

Crafting comfort food inside an architectural space of peace and connection.

From a Humble Cabin to a Modern Sensation

Founded with a deep passion for clean, cozy hospitality, Cabin Putih emerged as a sanctuary for food lovers. We set out to blend the rustic warmth of traditional culinary arts with modern, seamless digital dining workflows.

"We believe that premium dining isn't just about premium pricing; it's about the precision of fresh ingredients, high-tactile flavor arrays, and zero friction."

Whether you're stopping by to enjoy the serene slate-toned architecture of our physical tables, or ordering a hot wok-tossed noodle right to your door, our goal remains exactly the same.

Our Core Mission

To redefine localized dark-theme fast-casual dining across Malaysia by serving uncompromisingly fresh recipes through friction-free web pipelines powered by Oracle APEX layer mechanics[cite: 2].

Corporate Vision

Expanding from our physical base in Kedah to a multi-terminal nationwide digital franchise model, matching modern e-commerce transparency while honoring traditional cabin-cooked heritage.
